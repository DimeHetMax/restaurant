About Us

Oro Noir is a family restaurant built around fresh ingredients, generous hospitality, and dishes prepared with care. Every service is shaped to feel personal, relaxed, and quietly elegant from the first welcome to the final course.

Since opening in 1990, our menu has changed with the seasons, but our standards have stayed the same: balanced flavours, warm service, and memorable moments shared around the table.
