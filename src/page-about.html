About Us

Oro Noir is a family business which only uses the freshest ingredients. We aim to provide a dining experience with big flavours and a good atmosphere.

Every service is shaped by our belief that dining should feel personal, relaxed, and carefully considered. From the first welcome to the final course, our team focuses on warm hospitality, elegant presentation, and food that brings people together around the table.

Our History

The restaurant opened in 1990 as a small family dining room built around generous hospitality and honest cooking. Over the years, Oro Noir has grown into a place where guests come for carefully prepared dishes, thoughtful service, and an atmosphere that feels both elegant and welcoming.

Our menu has changed with the seasons, but our standards have stayed the same: fresh ingredients, balanced flavours, and care in every detail. Many of our guests have celebrated birthdays, anniversaries, and quiet evenings here, making the restaurant a part of their own stories too.
